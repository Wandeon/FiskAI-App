## Purpose

The DOOO Landing Page (/for/dooo) serves as a targeted entry point for d.o.o. (društvo s ograničenom odgovornošću) companies - limited liability companies primarily operating in Slovenia, Croatia, and other Balkan countries. This low-complexity, localized landing page addresses the specific needs, regulatory requirements, and pain points of d.o.o. entities, converting them into FiskAI users by demonstrating tailored accounting and invoicing solutions for their unique business context.

## D.O.O. Market Context

### What is a D.O.O.?

A d.o.o. (društvo s ograničenom odgovornošću) is a limited liability company used in Slovenia, Croatia, Serbia, and other Balkan countries. This is the most popular business structure for small and medium-sized enterprises in these regions due to simplified incorporation procedures and limited liability protection.

Reference: [Company Formation Croatia](https://companyformationcroatia.com/set-up-doo-ltd-croatia/)

### Regional Requirements

| Country  | Minimum Capital | Key Requirements                                                                 | Reference                                                                                                              |
| -------- | --------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Slovenia | EUR 7,500       | Min. 1 shareholder, min. 1 director (can be foreign), 22% corporate income tax   | [Company Formation Slovenia](https://companyformationslovenia.com/set-up-a-d-o-o-in-slovenia/)                         |
| Croatia  | EUR 2,500       | EUR 625 paid-up prior to registration, min. 1 director with minimum gross salary | [Expat In Croatia](https://www.expatincroatia.com/how-to-open-and-close-a-limited-liability-company-d-o-o-in-croatia/) |

### Accounting and Compliance Obligations

**Slovenia:**

- Annual financial statements within 3 months of fiscal year-end
- Compliance with Slovenian Accounting Standards or IFRS
- External accountant services typically required
- Reference: [Multilaw Slovenia LLC](https://multilaw.com/Multilaw/Multilaw/Global_Business_Entities_Guide/Slovenia_-_Limited_Liability_Company.aspx)

**Croatia:**

- Mandatory B2B e-invoicing from January 1, 2026 (Fiscalization 2.0)
- Dual accountancy principles (double-entry bookkeeping)
- Annual financial statements requiring audit by authorized public accountants
- Accountant fees: approximately EUR 2,500 annually for basic services
- Reference: [Croatia E-Invoicing Compliance](https://edicomgroup.com/blog/croatia-electronic-invoicing-b2b)
